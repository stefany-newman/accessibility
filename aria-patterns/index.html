Which sword is right for you?

Epee
Foil

The épée and foil are both thrusting weapons used in modern fencing, but they differ in rules and target areas. The épée is heavier and allows hits on the entire body, while the foil is lighter and restricts valid hits to the torso. Foil fencing uses "right of way" rules to determine which fencer scores, whereas épée does not. Both require precision and strategy but reward different styles of play.
